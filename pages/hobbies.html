My hobbies

I loved to draw and do crafts since childhood. Then at school, I became part of the pottery club. We held exhibitions and auctions of our works and presented them to the school's guests. So some of my works are now traveling around the world. I loved it so much that even after graduation, I sometimes came to the school's pottery workshop. I continued to do pottery and develop my skills at the university's pottery club.

Anime

One of my big hobbies is also watching anime. In 2017, I, several of my friends, and a professor founded an anime club. In this club, we not only watched anime together, but also created a magazine dedicated to anime and Japanese culture. Then we started selling it and gave all the collected money to charity, particularly animal shelters.
